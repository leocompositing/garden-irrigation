Key Observations
• SJ-512 swing joints (£612) are the killer — they're the single biggest line. Mike gets these at trade; retail UK price is ~£36 each. You could substitute a DIY 3-arm riser from ½" MI×MI nipples + ½" street elbows (~£8 each), saving ~£475, but joints are less flexible.
• Hunter HFR filter+regulator — no UK stockist found. Workarounds: (a) import via Amazon US (~£40–50 w/ shipping), (b) combine Hunter HY-100 wye filter (~£22) + Senninger PRF-40 regulator (~£18). Budget £40 either way.
• Philmac vs Tavlit — Mike proposed Plas-Fit Tavlit; DIY sticks with Philmac (your preference). Both are WRAS/KIWA; Philmac slightly pricier per fitting.
• Pipestock 403s — couldn't fetch directly during research. If their prices are sharper on MDPE & Philmac, subtotal could drop £30–60.
• Delivery — budget £15–25 per supplier. Worst case 6 suppliers ≈ £100 on top. Consolidate where possible (sprinkler-irrigation.co.uk can cover many lines).
• Trade verdict — Mike's £1,317.79 is competitive. Only source DIY if Mike becomes unavailable or drops critical items; the ~£290 premium is the price of full control, Philmac-everywhere, and avoiding dependency on one person.
